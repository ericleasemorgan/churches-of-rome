'''San Gabriele dellâ€™Addolorata a Torre Angela '''is a later 20th century public dependent chapel of the parish of [[Santi Simone e Giuda Taddeo a Torre Angela]] , and is located at Via Carlo Saraceni 10, in the suburb of [https://it.wikipedia.org/wiki/Torre_Angela Torre Angela] just north of the train station.

The dedication is to St [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gabriel_of_Our_Lady_of_Sorrows Gabriel of Our Lady of Sorrows].

== Appearance ==
The chapel occupies shop premises in the ground floor of a small three-storey apartment block.

It has no architectural interest. Look for the fascia saying ''Cappella S.Gabriele, ''and a faded print of the saint in a glazed frame.

== Liturgy ==
According to the Diocese (June 2018), the chapel is now closed during the week in summer.

There is a Mass on Fridays in winter at 9:00, and on Sundays and Solemnities all year at 10:00.[[Category:Catholic chapels in larger buildings]]
