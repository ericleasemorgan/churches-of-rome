'''Nostra Signora di Lourdes a Torrevecchia '''is a dependent chapel of the parish of [[Santa Maria della Presentazione a Torrevecchia]], and is located at Via Cristoforo Numai 15 in the south of Torrevecchia which in the [https://it.wikipedia.org/wiki/Primavalle Primavalle] quarter.

The dedication is to the [[Blessed Virgin Mary]], under her aspect of [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Our_Lady_of_Lourdes Our Lady of Lourdes].

It occupies a ground-floor shop unit in a three-storey Sixties apartment block, the first one on the left after turning off the Via di Torrevecchia into Via Cristoforo Numai. Look for the fascia saying ''Cappella Nostra Signora di Lourdes.''

Mass is celebrated (parish website, July 2018):

Weekdays 17:00 (not in summer);

Sundays and Solemnities 9:30.
