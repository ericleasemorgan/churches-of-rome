url= }} '''Santa Teresa del Bambin Gesù a Casal Fattoria '''is a public dependent chapel at Largo Christian Doppler 5 in the very new suburb of Casal Fattoria in the [https://it.wikipedia.org/wiki/Castel_di_Leva Castel di Leva] zone.

The dedication is to St [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Th%C3%A9r%C3%A8se_of_Lisieux Teresa of the Child Jesus]. 

This is a storefront chapel, occupying shop premises in the little street mall on the west side of the central piazza around which this small and isolated suburb is laid. It was opened in 2010.

The parish responsible is [[San Carlo Borromeo a Fonte Laurentina]].

Mass for Sunday is at 18:00 on Saturday, according to the parish website (July 2018).

In the unlikely event that you want to visit, the bus you want is the 797 from Laurentina metro station.
