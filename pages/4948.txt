'''Cappella Molino '''is a 20th century public chapel which (according to the Diocese) is located at Via Ardeatina km 10 just south of the junction with the [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Grande_Raccordo_Anulare Grande Raccordo Anulare] (Circonvallazione Meridionale). This is in the [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Grande_Raccordo_Anulare Castel di Leva] zone.

There is a famous hostelry and pizzeria located here called ''Al Molino'' (at the windmill). According to the ''Commune, ''the Petetti family which runs it also provided a chapel. The Diocese lists this on its web-page for the parish of [[Santuario della Madonna del Divino Amore|Santa Maria del Divino Amore a Castel di Leva]].

The access is from Via Giovan Domenico Nardo, presumably number 9 which is the location of the ''Al Molino ''car park. The overall edifice is a very irregularly built collection of disparate architectural units, some of which belong to other businesses.

There is no information online as regards liturgical activities here, if any take place nowadays.

The ''Al Molino ''web-page is [http://www.almolinoroma.com/Home.html here].
