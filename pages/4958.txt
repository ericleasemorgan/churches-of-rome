'''Oratorio di Beato Giacomo Cusmano '''is a 20th century college chapel or oratory located at Via di Torre Gaia 120, in the south of the suburban zone of [https://it.wikipedia.org/wiki/Torre_Gaia Torre Gaia] off the Via Casilina.

This is the convent chapel of the Roman headquarters of the [http://www.bocconisti.org/en/ Missionary Servants of the Poor], and is dedicated to their founder Bl [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Giacomo_Cusmano James Cusmano]. The premises apparently include a college, the ''[http://www.vicariatusurbis.org/?page_id=188&ID=2930 Collegio Cusmano], ''which has no Internet presence''.''

The chapel has no architectural identity and is private, but has been given a page on this Wiki because the dedication is unique in Rome.

The local parish church is [[Santa Maria Causa Nostrae Laetitiae]].
