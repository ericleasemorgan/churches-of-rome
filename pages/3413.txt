This is in the [https://it.wikipedia.org/wiki/La_Storta La Storta] zone.

The patron, St [http://it.wikipedia.org/wiki/Giovanni_Calabria John Calabria], was a holy priest of Verona who founded two religious congregations and who died in 1944.

This chapel is in the municipality of Rome, but belongs to the diocese of [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Suburbicarian_Diocese_of_Porto_e_Santa_Rufina Porto Santa Rufina].

It occupies part of the ground floor of a shabby three-storey apartment block, and is very easy to overlook. The entrance is in one end of the block.

Mass is celebrated (2016) at 17:00 on Thursdays and 9:00 on Sundays, but check the parish web-page [http://www.sacricuorilastorta.org/parrocchia/luoghi-di-culto/cappella-s-giovanni-calabria/ here].
