'''San Giuseppe Artigiano a Colle dei Pini '''is a late 20th century dependent chapel of the parish of [[San Romualdo Abate a Monte Migliore]] , and is located at Via Fosso di Radicelli 215, in [https://it.wikipedia.org/wiki/Colle_dei_Pini Colle dei Pini] on the Via Laurentina. This is in the [https://it.wikipedia.org/wiki/Castel_di_Decima Castel di Decima] zone.

The dedication is to St [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Saint_Joseph Joseph the Worker].

The location is an isolated suburb on the south edge of the municipal area, which was illegally developed in the later 20th century.

The chapel is a simple pre-fab with a pitched roof, part of a structure made up of several brought together as a little social centre. You will find it at the junction with the Via Casapulla.
