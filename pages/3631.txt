'''San Nicola a Tor Bella Monaca '''is a later 20th century public chapel located at Via Scicli 5, in the east of the suburb of [https://it.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tor_Bella_Monaca Tor Bella Monaca]. This is in the zone of [https://it.wikipedia.org/wiki/Torre_Angela Torre Angela], just north of the Via Casilina.

The dedication is to St [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Saint_Nicholas Nicholas of Myra].

== History ==
This is a dependent chapel of the parish of [[Santa Rita da Cascia a Torre Angela|Santa Rita a Torre Angela]] , opened in the Seventies by the look of it.

== Appearance ==
The chapel occupies a ground floor apartment of a three-storey apartment block, accessed by a short flight of steps. 

The entrance is only identifiable by means of a small wooden cross on the wall outside, and a brass plaque on the door.

== Liturgy ==
Mass is celebrated on Sundays and Solemnities (only) at 10:00, according to the Diocese (July 2018).[[Category:Catholic chapels in larger buildings]]
