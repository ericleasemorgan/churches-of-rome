'''Visitazione di Santa Maria a Dragona '''is a mid 20th century monastic chapel located at Via La Corsesca 72 [https://it.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dragona_(Roma) Dragona], which is the last suburb north of the Via Ostiense before you reach Ostia Antica. It is in the [https://it.wikipedia.org/wiki/Acilia_Nord Acilia Nord] zone.

The dedication is to the [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Visitation_(Christianity) Visitation of Our Lady].

== History ==
The chapel belongs to a monastery of Visitation nuns, which is an enclosed order. It was founded here at the end of the Fifties, when the suburb was being developed.

It is within the parish of [[Santa Maria Regina dei Martiri in Via Ostiense]] , but has no public liturgical profile.

== Access ==
The monastery is up a fairly long driveway, and is invisible from the street. It is private.
