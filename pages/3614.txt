'''Santa Maria Maddalena agli Arcacci '''is a 20th century dependent chapel of the parish of [[Santi Simone e Giuda Taddeo a Torre Angela]] , and is located at Via Trinitapoli 88 in the north part of the suburb of [https://it.wikipedia.org/wiki/Torre_Angela Torre Angela], near the junction of the Via Prenestina with the [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Grande_Raccordo_Anulare Grande Raccordo Anulare] (''Circonvallazione Orientale'').

The dedication is to St [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mary_Magdalene Mary Magdalen].

== Appearance ==
It is a little, nondescript flat-roofed pale orange building behind a railing gate at the north-west end of the street, which is a dead end here. Look for the large cross perched on the right hand corner of the roof. 

== Liturgy ==
According to the Diocese (June 2018), the chapel is now closed during the week in summer.

Mass is celebrated on Wednesdays in winter at 16:30, and on Sundays and Solemnities at 9:00 and 11:00 (winter only).[[Category:Catholic churches]]
