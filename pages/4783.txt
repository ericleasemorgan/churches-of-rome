'''San Timoteo della Società San Paolo '''is a mid 20th century public dependent chapel of the parish of [[Santa Maria Regina degli Apostoli alla Montagnola]] , and is located at Via Alessandro Severo 52 in the south of Garbatella.

The dedication is to St [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Saint_Timothy Timothy].

This is the house chapel of the convent of the [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Society_of_St._Paul Society of St Paul], which administers the church and parish of ''Santa Maria Regina degli Apostoli. ''The large complex includes the main church, and several large convent blocks. The northernmost of the latter has an entrance off the street, reached by stairs down into a sunken area. Look for a doorway with an inscription ''Società San Paolo.''

There is no separate external identity for the chapel.

Mass is celebrated (parish website, July 2018):

Weekdays 8:00, 17:00, 18:00 (Saturdays only);

Sundays and Solemnities 8:00, 9:30, 11:00, 18:00.
