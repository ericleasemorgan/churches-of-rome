'''Resurrezione di Nostro Signore Gesù Cristo a Via Prenestina '''is a dependent chapel of the parish of [[San Luca a Via Prenestina]] , and is located at Via Anacapri 11, just south of the Via Prenestina in the [https://it.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prenestino-Labicano Prenestino-Labicano] quarter.

The dedication is the Resurrection of Our Lord Jesus Christ.

The chapel is in the ground floor of an apartment block.

Mass is celebrated here on Tuesdays, Thursdays, Fridays and Saturdays at 18:00 and on Sundays and Solemnities at 10:30. This chapel is closed in July and August.
